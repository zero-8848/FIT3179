How Road Death Rates Relate to Population Density

This scatter plot illustrates the relationship between a country's population density and its road death rate. Interestingly, the data suggests an inverse relationship: as population density increases, the death rate tends to decrease. This counterintuitive trend may be attributed to various factors such as better infrastructure, stricter traffic regulations, or more public transportation options in densely populated areas. It underscores the importance of understanding the multifaceted factors that contribute to road safety.
